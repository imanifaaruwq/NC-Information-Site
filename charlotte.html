Charlotte, North Carolina

Charlotte is the most populous city in North Carolina, located in the Piedmont region. Between 2004 and 2014, Charlotte was ranked as the country' fasted growing metro area. Charlotte is home to the corporate headquarters of Bank of America, Truist Financial, and the East Coast headquarters of Wells Fargo.
